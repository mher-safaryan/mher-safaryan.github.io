Bio

I am a postdoctoral fellow at KAUST, working on Optimization for Machine Learning in the group supervised by Prof. Peter Richtárik. Previously, I worked with Prof. Diogo Gomes on Computer Algebra for Differential Equations.

I did my PhD in Mathematics at Yerevan State University, Armenia under the supervision of Prof. Grigori Karagulyan.
